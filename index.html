What is Fantasy Cricket? Complete Guide

Understanding Fantasy Cricket — A Complete Beginner's Guide

Fantasy cricket is an online sports game where participants create virtual teams composed of real-life cricket players. Your virtual team earns points based on the actual statistical performance of those players in real matches.

How Fantasy Cricket Scoring Works

📊 Standard Dream11 T20 Scoring

  • Batting: +1 per run, +1 boundary bonus, +2 six bonus, +8 fifty, +16 century, -2 duck
  • Bowling: +25 per wicket, +8 maiden, +8 3WK bonus, +16 5WK bonus
  • Fielding: +8 catch, +12 stumping, +12 direct run-out, +6 indirect run-out
  • Captain: 2× all points · Vice-Captain: 1.5× all points

What is Fantasy Cricket?

Fantasy cricket is an online strategy game where you build a virtual team of real cricket players and earn points based on their actual match-day performance.

📊 Dream11 T20 Scoring — Key Rules

  • Batting: +1 per run, +1 boundary, +2 six, +8 fifty, +16 century, -2 duck
  • Bowling: +25 per wicket, +8 maiden, +8 3WK bonus, +16 5WK bonus
  • Fielding: +8 catch, +12 stumping, +12 direct run-out, +6 indirect
  • Captain: 2× ALL points · Vice-Captain: 1.5× ALL points

Team Composition Rules

  • 1–4 Wicketkeeper-Batsmen · 3–6 Batsmen · 1–4 All-rounders · 3–6 Bowlers
  • Maximum 7 players from one team
